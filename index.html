P3 Protagonist

Savior

The protagonist of Persona 3, bearing the persona called "Orpheus" and ultimate persona called "Messiah". Their parents died in a car crash, harboring "Death" inside of them because of a fight nearby. Sacrifices themselves in the end in order to save the world from "Death".

Mitsuru Kirijo

The Imperious Queen Of Executions

Student Council President of Gekkoukan High, bearing the persona called "Penthesilea" and ultimate persona called "Artemisia". She is the only daughter and sole heir to the Kirijo Group, an organization led by her father who tragically passed away from "sickness".

Koromaru

The Loyal Hound of the Shirne

Former guard dog on Naganaki Shrine, currently a guard dog on Iwatodai Dorm, bearing the ultimate persona called "Cerberus" His owner tragically passed away in an accident, he goes on walks by himself using the same route him and his owner took when he was still alive.

Aigis

The Heartless Armed Angel

A specialized weapon made to kill "Shadows", bearing the persona called "Palladion" and ultimate persona called "Athena". She is a robot who learns the true way of life and what's it like to become human, after being defeated by her number one enemy.

Junpei Iori

The Mighty Strike-Out Slugger

A class clown bearing the persona called "Hermes" and ultimate persona called "Trismegistus". Fun and caring guy that lost the love of his life, resulting in him turning his life around and starting to appreciate everyone around him, turning mature and even responsible for the actions he does.
